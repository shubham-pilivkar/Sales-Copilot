## Name
Sales Copilot — AI Sales Assistant for Google Meet

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Real-time AI sales coaching during Google Meet calls. Get live nudges, objection handling, and conversation intelligence.

## Detailed Description
Sales Copilot is your AI-powered sales coach that sits alongside you during Google Meet calls and provides real-time nudges to help you close more deals.

**How it works:**
1. Open a Google Meet call
2. Enter your prospect's email
3. Click "Start Sales Assistant"
4. Get live coaching nudges as the conversation unfolds

**What you get:**
• Objection handling suggestions when prospects push back
• Competitor battle cards when rivals are mentioned
• Discovery questions to uncover pain points
• Talk ratio warnings when you're speaking too much
• Pricing concern detection with ROI reframing
• Meeting stage tracking (opening → discovery → presenting → closing)
• Prospect brief card with company context
• Post-meeting summary with action items

**Key features:**
• Works in real-time (2-5 second latency)
• Non-intrusive overlay — minimizable floating panel
• Click-to-copy suggested responses
• Dismiss nudges you don't need
• Keyboard shortcuts (Esc to dismiss)
• Secure — audio never stored, only processed live
• Privacy-first — visible indicator when active

**This is a copilot, not an autopilot.** You stay in control of the conversation. The AI suggests — you decide.
