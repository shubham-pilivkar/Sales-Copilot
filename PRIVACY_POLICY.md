# Sales Copilot — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

## What This Extension Does
Sales Copilot provides real-time AI coaching nudges during Google Meet sales calls.

## Data Collection
- **Audio:** Captured only after explicit user consent + clicking "Start." Processed in real-time, never stored as files.
- **Transcripts:** Retained 24 hours for summary generation, then deleted.
- **Account:** Email and name for authentication.
- **Prospect email:** Provided by user for context enrichment.

## Data NOT Collected
- No audio recordings saved
- No video/screen capture
- No browsing history
- No data sold to third parties

## When Capture Occurs
- Only after clicking "Start Sales Assistant"
- Visible pulsing indicator when active
- Stops immediately on "Stop" or meeting end

## Security
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS/WSS)
- JWT authentication
- Server-side consent verification

## Your Rights
- Access, delete, or export your data at any time
- Revoke audio consent at any time
