🎯 Sales Copilot needs audio access

To provide real-time sales nudges, the assistant needs to listen to your meeting audio.

A visible indicator will show when the assistant is active

What we capture:

  • Meeting tab audio (participant voices)
  • Your microphone (your voice)

What we do NOT do:

  • Audio is never stored — processed in real-time then discarded
  • No recording is saved to disk
  • Capture stops immediately when you click Stop
